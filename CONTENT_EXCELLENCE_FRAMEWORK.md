## Research Standards

### **Citation Requirements:**
- **Primary Sources:** Peer-reviewed journals only
- **Recency:** 70% of studies from last 3 years  
- **Authority:** Recognized medical journals (NEJM, JAMA, Nature, etc.)
- **Relevance:** Studies on similar populations (women 45+, relevant conditions)
- **Access:** Working PubMed or journal links required

### **Expert Standards:**
- **Credentials:** MD, PhD, or equivalent in relevant field
- **Experience:** Minimum 5 years clinical/research experience
- **Specialization:** Expertise specifically relevant to article topic
- **Transparency:** Any conflicts of interest disclosed
